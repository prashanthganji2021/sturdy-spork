Naukri Cookie Setup

1. Open a new tab and log in to naukri.com.
2. Open the browser console (Ctrl + Shift + I) and run: await this.cookieStore.getAll()
3. Right-click the output and select "Copy".
4. Paste the copied cookie data into the above field.
